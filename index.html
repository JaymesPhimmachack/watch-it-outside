Watch it Outside

Film Festival

About Us

Clara Dunn is the founder of Watch it outside events Co. and is organizing an outdoor film festival in Boston this year called ‘Watch it outside Boston edition’. She hopes to select and show films from August 5th to 8th in Boston Public Garden.

She wants to create an event that shows that films can appeal to audiences of all ages.

News & Announcements

My company, "Watch it Outside" just got the approval to show movies in Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. We can't wait to help the public discover awesome films!

Shows will be free and open to everyone, but I want people to be able to pre-register so that I'm able to estimate how many people might come and so that we can close registration if it reaches the venue’s capacity of 1,500 people.

Amazing Films at Watch It Outside Film Fest August 5th to 8th

Wake Up

In a short film directed by Olivia Wilde, Margaret Qualley stars as a woman awakened to a world she doesn't recognize, one where people are more engaged with screens and devices than with each other. She sets out on a journey to find connection in a disconnected world and relearn what it means to be human.

Showtime: August 5th, 6pm - 8pm

Beast Beast

Three interwoven stories of youth navigating identity, first love, petty crime, and gun violence in a Southern, American town.

Showtime: August 5th, 8:30pm - 12pm

Schemers

His soccer career over, Davie starts promoting gigs in Dundee, Scotland with his friends John and Scot – leading to a hugely ambitious IRON MAIDEN show. Out of his depth and deep in debt with FERGIE, a gangster of legendary violence, Davie needs to pull off the biggest scheme of his life. BASED ON A TRUE STORY.

Showtime: August 6th, 6pm - 8pm

The Illegal

A gritty realistic story about a young film school student from middle class India who's forced to drop out to support his family while staying in the United States as an undocumented worker.

Showtime: August 6th, 8:30pm - 12pm

Angela

Angela is a 13-year-old peasant girl whose virginity is bought from her father by Calixto, a landowner in the area. Angela is the story of how the birth of a woman ends up becoming the curse of being at the mercy of what men and social rules decide about the body and life of women

Showtime: August 7th, 6pm - 8pm

Speed Of Time

Future cop Johnny Killfire (John Hennigan, WWE) must go back in time and team up with his former self (Sean Marquette, The Goldbergs) in order to stop the TimeBorgs from getting their hands on an app that could break the space-time continuum by delivering pizzas into the past...before they were even ordered.

Showtime: August 7th, 6pm - 8pm

Miseducation of Bindu

When a bullied Indian teen forges her mother’s signature to test out of high school, she discovers she must pay a test fee by 7th period, leaving her no choice but to turn to the students she desperately wants to leave.

Showtime: August 7th, 8:30pm - 12pm

Bastard's Road

A Veteran’s life spirals out of control after coming home from war. After hitting rock bottom, he decides to walk across the country. Across 5,800 miles he wrestles with inner demons, visits members of his fellow 2/4 Marines - known as the The Magnificent Bastards - and families of their fallen. Along the way, Jon discovers a mission even bigger than his own redemption.

Showtime: August 8th, 6pm - 8pm

Stalking Chernobyl: Exploration After Apocalypse

Three decades after the world's most infamous nuclear disaster, wildlife has returned in the absence of human settlements. Meanwhile, illegal hiking adventurers known as "stalkers," extreme sports aficionados, artists, and tour companies have begun to explore anew the ghostly, post-apocalyptic landscape.

Showtime: August 8th, 8:30pm - 12pm
